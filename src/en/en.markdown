subtitle: Ultimate Frisbee in the heart of Paris!
overview: |
  PUC Ultimate brings together several women’s, open, and mixed teams that compete each year in national and international championships (outdoor, indoor, and beach).

  Each team has its own training slot and a dedicated coach, ensuring personalized support and continuous progress. Within the club culture, coaches regularly share exercises, organize friendly matches, and exchange experiences to help the whole group grow.

  This collaborative strength makes PUC Ultimate a unique club: a large collective where everyone pushes each other higher, in a spirit of diversity, teamwork, and performance to proudly represent the club on French and international fields.

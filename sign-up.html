The Ah Ouh Puc Ultimate seasons begins on September, 1st and ends on August, 31. To join the club, you need to sign up in September or October. It's also possible to sign up for a half season (September, 1st – February, 28, or March, 1st – August, 31).

Before signing up, we encourage you to try out a few practices to get an idea of the club and meet some of the players.

Fee

The sign-up fee includes the FFDF licence which is required to play the championships.

full season half season
Standard € 175 € 105
Student (max. 28) € 145 € 105
Already licenced by FFDF in another club € 145 € 80

Sign-up procedure

  1. Download and complete the Ah Ouh Puc information form. Send it to and tell us what is your current level and ultimate experience.
  2. Wait for the confirmation email.
  3. Print and complete the PUC sign-up form. Hand it back at the practice along with:
    • 2 ID photos with your name written on the back.
    • A doctor's certificate stating the "non contre-indication à la pratique des sports de disques volants en compétition". See the doctor's certificate model. If you don't have a general practionner in Paris, ask anyone at the practice.
    • The cheque to the order of "PUC". If you want, you can pay in two separate cheques: one of € 100 which will be cashed immediately, and another one of the remaining which will be cashed by the end of January. You still need to hand both cheques when you sign up. If you don't have a cheque-book, bring cash and we will make a cheque for you.
    • If you qualify for the student fee, a photocopy of your student card.
